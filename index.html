UDISE Plus 2025: India’s Unified School Data Management Platform

The education landscape in India has been transformed with digital tools that simplify school administration and data collection. One of the most important initiatives in this domain is UDISE plus (Unified District Information System for Education Plus). Developed and maintained by the Department of School Education and Literacy under the Ministry of Education, UDISE+ provides a centralized platform for collecting, maintaining, and analyzing accurate school data across India.

What is UDISE Plus?

UDISE Plus is an upgraded digital platform from the earlier UDISE system. It provides a comprehensive, real-time, and reliable database of schools, students, teachers, and infrastructure. Unlike previous systems, UDISE+ allows instant updates, automated reports, and data analytics, which are essential for effective decision-making in education.

Key Objectives of UDISE Plus

  • Maintain a standardized, centralized school database nationwide.
  • Provide accurate data for evidence-based policy making.
  • Monitor student performance, teacher efficiency, and school infrastructure.
  • Reduce duplication and errors through digital entry and validation.
  • Support planning at school, district, state, and national levels.

Who Uses UDISE Plus?

The platform serves multiple users in India’s education ecosystem:

  • School Administrators: For maintaining school records and reporting data.
  • Teachers: To enter student performance, attendance, and class data.
  • District and State Officials: For monitoring school progress and infrastructure.
  • Researchers and Policy Makers: To analyze trends and improve educational outcomes.

Accessing UDISE Plus

Authorized users can log in via the official portal: https://udisepluslogin.com/. The login steps are:

  1. Visit the official UDISE Plus website.
  2. Enter the school or district code assigned by the education department.
  3. Provide your username and password.
  4. Complete the captcha if prompted.
  5. Click the login button to access the dashboard and manage school data.

Main Features of UDISE Plus

UDISE+ offers several features that improve school data management:

  • Centralized Database: One platform for all schools across India.
  • Real-Time Updates: Instant updates reduce delays and improve data accuracy.
  • Automated Reports: Generates school, teacher, and student reports automatically.
  • Secure Access: Protects sensitive data with login credentials and encryption.
  • Data Analytics: Provides insights into enrollment, learning outcomes, and infrastructure gaps.
  • Teacher and Student Tracking: Helps monitor attendance, performance, and progression.

UDISE Plus Reports

The system produces multiple reports for administrators and policymakers:

Report Type Description
School Summary Report Overview of enrollment, teachers, and infrastructure for each school.
Student Enrollment Report Detailed enrollment by class, gender, and category.
Teacher Profile Report Qualifications, experience, and attendance of teachers.
Infrastructure Report Facilities available at schools, including classrooms, labs, toilets, and libraries.

Benefits of UDISE Plus

  • Improved Data Accuracy: Reduces errors through validation and real-time entry.
  • Evidence-Based Planning: Helps policymakers make informed decisions.
  • Transparency: Promotes accountability in reporting and monitoring.
  • Monitoring Educational Outcomes: Tracks student learning and teacher effectiveness.
  • Resource Allocation: Supports better distribution of educational resources based on data insights.

Challenges in UDISE Plus

  • Training staff for accurate and consistent data entry.
  • Ensuring internet access, especially in remote schools.
  • Maintaining privacy and security of sensitive school data.
  • Regular verification and correction of submitted data.

Trusted Resources

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • Who can access UDISE Plus? Authorized school administrators, teachers, and district/state officials.
  • How often should data be updated? Schools should update data at least once per academic year or as directed.
  • Is the data secure? Yes, the system uses encrypted storage and secure login.
  • How to correct errors? Schools submit corrections which are verified by district officials.
